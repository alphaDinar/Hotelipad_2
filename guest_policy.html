GUEST POLICY Welcome to HoteliPad, an online hotel booking platform. We want to ensure that all guests have a pleasant and enjoyable stay. Please take a moment to review our guest policy:
1. Age Requirement

Guests must be at least 18 years old to book a hotel room through our website. Any guests under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult.

2. Identification

All guests must present a valid government-issued photo ID at check-in. The name on the ID must match the name on the reservation.

3. Payment and Authorization

Guests must provide a valid credit card at the time of booking. The credit card will be authorized for the full amount of the reservation, including any taxes and fees. The hotel may also require a security deposit or pre-authorization on the credit card at check-in.

4. Smoking Policy

All hotel rooms and public areas are non-smoking. Smoking is strictly prohibited. Guests who violate the smoking policy will be subject to a cleaning fee.

5. Noise Policy

Guests must respect the privacy and comfort of other guests. Excessive noise is not allowed, especially after 10 PM. Any guests who disturb other guests will be asked to leave without a refund.

6. Pet Policy

Pets are not allowed in hotel rooms, with the exception of service animals. Guests who bring pets to the hotel will be subject to a cleaning fee.

7. Extra Guests

Hotel rooms are priced based on the number of guests allowed. Any additional guests must be registered and approved by the hotel, and may be subject to additional fees.

8. Damage and Theft

Guests are responsible for any damage or theft to hotel property. Any guests who damage hotel property will be charged for the cost of repairs or replacement.

9. Housekeeping

Hotel rooms will be cleaned daily by housekeeping staff. Guests should keep their valuables and personal belongings secure.

10. Check-out Time

Check-out time is typically 11 AM. Late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional fees.
